Instructions


Your Group

You are in a group with 5 other people. These people have been chosen randomly from the American pool of Prolific.

Your Task

In this study you will complete two tasks. Each task has 3 rounds which yields 6 rounds in total. Each of these rounds will take at most 2 minutes.

Your Payment

You will earn a participation payment of {{C.Participation_fee}}$ for completing the study. On top of your participation payment, you can earn a bonus payment of up to {{C.Bonus_fee_max}}$. One of the 6 rounds will be chosen randomly to determine your bonus payment. This round is called the Bonus-Relevant Round. The method used to determine the bonus payment depends on the Bonus-Relevant Round. Before each round, we will explain how the bonus payment is determined. While the participation payment is paid upon completion, you will receive your bonus payment within 2 weeks of completing the study.

These instructions will always be available to you at the bottom of the screen by clicking the "Instructions" button.
