Instructions


Your group

You form a group with 5 other people. These people have been chosen randomly from the pool of other participants of this study. All participants of the study are US adults.

Your task

In this study you will play two games. Each game has 3 rounds which yields 6 rounds in total. Each of these rounds will take at most 2 minutes.

Your payment

You will earn a participation payment of {{C.Participation_fee}}$ for completing the study. On top of your participation payment, you can earn a bonus payment of up to {{C.Bonus_fee_max}}$. One of the 6 rounds will be chosen randomly to determine your bonus payment. This round is called the bonus-relevant round. Before each round, we will explain how the bonus payment is determined. While the participation payment is paid upon completion, you will receive your bonus payment within a week of completing the study.

These instructions will always be available to you at the bottom of the screen by clicking the "Instructions" button.
